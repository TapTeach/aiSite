Have an idea? Prototype Submission

VMLY&R Misfit Concepts

As part of VMLY&R’s growing effort to enrich the client base and bring tangible examples of innovation to the forefront, the Mobile Apps and Emerging Tech group will be offering a second chance to ideas and concepts from all teams. If you have an idea you are still passionate about, but aren’t sure where it fits, you could be in luck!

Examples of ideas that qualify:

• Client Concepts that didn’t make the cut.

• Passion Projects waiting for a campaign.

• New tech you are dying to try.

• Forward thinking ideas that solve big problems.

Once per quarter by a panel of judges comprised of innovative minds across VMLY&R, using all submissions available, will choose projects to move forward. Judging will occur on March 15, May 15, July 15, and September 15.
