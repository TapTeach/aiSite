Meet the Team

View small bio summary for Eric Campbell

Eric Campbell

As VML’s President, Eric provides strategic leadership across our global offices, ensuring continued growth for current client engagements and new business efforts. He is known for his inventive ways of connecting with client partners to help them navigate the constantly-changing marketing landscape.

Since joining VML in 1999, Eric’s been pivotal in the evolution of our capabilities across departments and client teams. He applies his strategic, creative, technological and operational experience to deliver unique solutions. Eric has been central to expanding the VML footprint as the leading strategic partner for the world’s most forward-thinking brands.

Eric has served on the VML Executive Leadership Team since 2007, most recently as chief business officer, where he was responsible for the agency's business development practice. He led the launch of VML Seattle in 2004 with the Microsoft business, and has been integral in VML’s inclusion in other Team WPP operations.

In his free time, Eric enjoys spending time with his family. He is also an avid baseball fan and enjoys the lifelong challenge of improving his golf game. He graduated from the University of Central Missouri with a Bachelor of Science in graphic arts technology management.

Eric Campbell

View small bio summary for Rosanne Johnson

Rosanne Johnson

Rosanne joined Team Colgate/VML in January 2012 as the Global Digital Lead. Previously she spent five years as a significant contributor to the development of Ryan Partnership’s digital and CRM expertise. She managed the overall digital strategy, CRM and brand mission work for Dove U.S. Masterbrand and was a key player in Dove Global digital initiatives. Under her leadership, the Dove brand implemented several strategic initiatives in .com, media, mobile, ecommerce, research and CRM/social.

Before joining Ryan, Rosanne was Marketing Director at Byte Interactive/Story Worldwide, where she oversaw the digital strategy and CRM programs for Unilever Foods and Coty Beauty brands. She has a long history in data and direct marketing, having held positions at Nielsen and Donnelley Marketing. During her career, Rosanne has worked with consumer packaged goods, continuity marketing, retail and financial companies. Rosanne grew up in Connecticut and earned a bachelor’s degree and MBA from the University of Connecticut. She lives in Fairfield, CT with her husband, son and daughter.

Rosanne has a long consumer history with BMW – she has owned 8 BMWs, is a long-time member of the BMW car club, and is the proud wife and mother to BMW driving school certificate holders.

Rosanne Johnson

Miriam Raisner

Miriam is a Director on the Strategy & insights team. She came to VML in 2015 through WPP’s MBA Fellowship, and has served as a Director since 2016.

Miriam currently leads planning work across several accounts, including New Balance. Since joining VML, she has worked on accounts such as J.P. Morgan Chase, Kellogg’s Special K, and Shiseido. Her passion is drawing meaningful insights from unexpected places—anywhere from social behavior to behavioral science—and seeing them through to executions that work hard for brands.

Christina Miller
